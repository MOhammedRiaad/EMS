## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. You'll receive an invitation email from your EMS studio
2. Click the **Set Up Your Account** link
3. Enter your password and personal details
4. You're ready to start booking!

### Logging In

1. Go to `http://your-studio.com/client`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Login**

## Booking Sessions

### How to Book

1. Click **Book Session** from the dashboard or navigation
2. Select your preferred:
   - **Date**: Choose from the calendar
   - **Time**: Available slots are shown in green (based on your home studio)
   - **Coach** (optional): Pick your preferred trainer.
     > **Note**: Only coaches from your studio who match your gender preference will appear.
3. Review your selection
4. Click **Confirm Booking**

> **Tip**: Slots marked in gray are unavailable due to full bookings or studio closure.

### Booking Rules

- Book up to **4 weeks** in advance
- You need an **active package** with remaining sessions
- Sessions are **60 minutes** by default

## Managing Your Sessions

### Viewing Your Schedule

1. Go to **My Sessions**
2. See upcoming and past sessions
3. Filter by date range or status

### Cancelling a Session

1. Select the session you want to cancel
2. Click **Cancel Session**
3. Confirm the cancellation

> **Important**: Cancellations must be made at least **48 hours** before the session. Late cancellations may result in session deduction.

### Rescheduling

1. Cancel the existing session
2. Book a new session at your preferred time

## Waiting List

### When Sessions Are Full

If your preferred time slot is fully booked:

1. Click **Join Waiting List**
2. Select your preferred time
3. Wait for notification when a slot opens
4. Book quickly when notified!

### Managing Your Waitlist Requests

1. Go to **My Waiting List**
2. View all pending requests
3. Remove requests you no longer need

## FAQ

### How do I reset my password?

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check your inbox for the reset link
4. Create a new password

### What happens if I miss a session?

No-shows may be counted as used sessions depending on your studio's policy. Always cancel at least 48 hours in advance.

### How do I purchase more sessions?

Contact your studio directly to purchase additional packages. They can assign them to your account immediately.

### Can I change my coach?

Yes! When booking, you can select any available coach. If you don't have a preference, leave it as "Any Available Coach."

### How do I contact my studio?

Your studio's contact information is available in the **Contact** section of the app.
